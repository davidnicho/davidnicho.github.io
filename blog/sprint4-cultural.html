• Discuss how understanding the principles of neuroplasticity benefits people.


Life is not static and changes all the time, just as the mind. Starve yourself for a day and new neural pathways will be formed so you can figure out new ways to find food and in the same way, new information is processed by old processes but when that is not working, brains, not being static will create new pathways to process the information.
