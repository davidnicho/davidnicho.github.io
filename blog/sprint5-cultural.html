• Why do you think we, a programming school, are spending so much time focusing on core learning in a web development bootcamp course?


The core is clearly about testing the will and the adaptability into a field that has monotony at its core. Not that it is boring but that it can be very repetitive and can drive you mad. It is similar in the way that editing might sound cool but you then have to archive and organise everything you have worked on for a time when you may but probably we never work on again. I have seen breakdowns and tantrums in the work place and they are stem from a lack of communication and people not feeling valued or seeing their self-worth. Another reason is likely that many of those that have, in the past, chosen to be in this area have been what was always considered nerdy, lame wads or poindexters. The reality however is more that those that are autistic or have aspergers, in the 90s and above on the spectrum, tend to be drawn to these fields due to the innate lack of ability to communicate effectively and not able to see or read people as a normie can.
